About Ronnie James Dio

Though primarily known for his work in the 80s, Ronnie James Dio's influence persisted throughout the 90s with his powerful vocals and thematic music that continued to inspire rock musicians and fans alike.

Van Halen in the 90s

Van Halen continued to impact the rock scene in the 90s with albums like "For Unlawful Carnal Knowledge" which brought a new energy to their music and live performances.

Queen's Legacy

The 90s saw Queen navigating the loss of Freddie Mercury, with their music reaching new audiences and continuing to influence the world of rock.
